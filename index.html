About Our School

Nurturing Excellence Since 1996

Our History

Founded in 1996, Sri Sai Vidyalaya High School has been at the forefront of quality education for three decades. What started as a modest campus with 50 students has blossomed into a premier educational hub, offering world-class infrastructure and holistic learning approaches.

Our Mission

To cultivate academic excellence, foster creativity, and instill strong ethical values in students, enabling them to become responsible global citizens.

Our Vision

To be a leading global educational institution recognized for nurturing innovative thinkers, outstanding leaders, and lifelong learners.

"Education is not the learning of facts, but the training of the mind to think. At SSVHS, we inspire minds to reach their full potential."

Our Premium Facilities

Smart Classrooms

Air-conditioned rooms equipped with high-definition digital smart boards and ergonomic furniture.

Computer Labs

High-speed internet workstations with modern software suites for computer education and project work.

Robotics Lab

Dedicated innovation hub for learning mechanical assemblies, programming microcontrollers, and IoT.

Science Laboratories

Fully certified Physics, Chemistry, and Biology laboratories equipped with premium testing gear.

Digital Library

An extensive storehouse of over 50,000 physical books and digital database access terminals.

Spacious arena for badminton, table tennis, gymnastics, chess, and dynamic board games.

Standard professional athletics tracks, turf football ground, cricket pitches, and basketball courts.
